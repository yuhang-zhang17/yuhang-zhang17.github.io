## Engineering Scalable District Energy Systems for Building Electrification

I develop high-fidelity modeling and model predictive control (MPC) strategies 
for district-scale heating and cooling networks.

My work integrates physics-based simulation, data-driven modeling, 
and system-level optimization to improve efficiency, flexibility, 
and decarbonization potential of thermal energy infrastructure.

**Focus Areas:**  
District energy systems • Thermal microgrids • MPC • Hybrid modeling • Electrification
